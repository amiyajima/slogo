Detailed Description

Hold all information and behaviors for the turtle in SLogo. The turtle is capable of turning and moving, as well as hiding. It hold a pen which it can raise and lower, altering when lines are drawn. It always begins at the origin and can return home.

Author
Ethan Chang
Eli Lichtenberg
